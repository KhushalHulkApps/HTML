helping pets on better oral health

Proven. Trusted. Innovative.

Dr. George K. Stookey, a leading dental scientist, developed patented approaches to reduce the formation of dental calculus, improving the dental health of companion animals. As a result of his numerous studies, Tartar Shield was developed.

Our products were developed to prevent periodontal disease before it starts and help pets on their journey towards better oral health. They are clinically proven to clean your pet’s teeth, freshen their breath, and reduce the build-up of plaque, tartar and gingivitis.
